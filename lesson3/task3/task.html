# Maximum Advertisement Revenue Find the maximum dot product of two sequences of numbers. * Input: Two sequences of $n$ positive integers: $price_1,\dots,price_n$ and $clicks_1,\dots,clicks_n$. * Output: The maximum value of $price_1 \cdot c_1 + \dots+price_n \cdot c_n$, where $c_1,\dots,c_n$ is a permutation of $clicks_1,\dots,clicks_n$.
